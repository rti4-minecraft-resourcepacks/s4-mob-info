Mob Tier Extra Stats & Skills

How to Use: This page details the extra stats and skills that are typically added to mobs as they increase in tier. Use this as a guideline for balancing and designing new mobs.

Typical Skills by Tier

TierSkill Examples
CommonNone or very basic (e.g., Leap, Simple Arrow)
RareMinor buffs, weak debuffs, simple AoE
EliteModerate AoE, crowd control, self-heal, moderate buffs
ChampionStrong AoE, summoning, strong debuffs, shield skills
MythicMultiple strong skills, phase changes, high-impact abilities
AscendedBoss-level skills, invulnerability phases, resurrection, unique mechanics

Best Practices

  • Gradually introduce new mechanics as players progress through tiers.
  • Ensure higher-tier mobs feel distinct and challenging, not just stat increases.
  • Test new skills in isolation before adding to boss encounters.
  • Document all custom skills for future reference.
